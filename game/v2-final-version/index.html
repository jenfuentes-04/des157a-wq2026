Who goes first?

How to Play

There are two players. You get to choose which player gets to roll the dice first. The total of the roll is added to the current player's score, unless either die comes up as a "one". If this happens, this player's turn is over, and it is the other player's turn.

After each roll, the current player can either roll again or pass to the other player. The first player to get 30 points or higher wins.

Oh, and if you roll two "ones" (snake eyes), your current score gets zeroed out. So don't do that!
